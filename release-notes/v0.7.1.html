Enterprise MVP Baseline

🚀 OpenS2P v0.7.1

Demo Release Release Date: July 2026

OpenS2P v0.7.1 establishes the first complete open-source Source-to-Pay platform baseline — procurement workflows, enterprise security, integrations, analytics, and a modern web experience, all in one release.

Platform Overview

A modern procurement workspace

Built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, OpenS2P gives every role — from requesters to finance — a dashboard suited to their job, with role-based navigation throughout.

  • Executive Dashboard
  • Supplier Management
  • Contract Management

  • Approval Inbox
  • Analytics

Source-to-Pay Capabilities

Supplier Management

Onboard and manage suppliers with a full profile, status tracking, and the early foundations for performance and risk scoring.

  • Supplier onboarding
  • Supplier profiles
  • Status management
  • Supplier performance foundation
  • Supplier risk foundation

Contract Lifecycle Management

A central contract repository with lifecycle tracking, renewal management, and approval workflow support.

  • Contract repository
  • Contract lifecycle tracking
  • Renewal management foundation
  • Approval workflow support

Procurement

Create purchase requisitions with line items, route them through approvals, and convert them into purchase orders with full lifecycle management.

Request→ Approval→ Purchase Order→ Receiving→ Invoice

Invoice Management

Process invoices with match-status tracking, the foundation for exception handling, and payment status tracking.

  • Invoice processing
  • Match status tracking
  • Exception handling foundation
  • Payment status tracking

Workflow Engine

An enterprise workflow service powers dynamic, multi-step approvals across the platform — with approval tasks, full history, and event publishing so other systems can react to what happens.

PR Created→ Manager Approval→ Finance Approval→ Approved

Analytics Intelligence

A new analytics platform surfaces spend, supplier, and contract insights out of the box.

  • Spend dashboards
  • Supplier analytics
  • Contract analytics
  • Cycle-time metrics
  • KPI engine
  • Report exports

Integration Framework

An enterprise connector architecture lays the groundwork for connecting OpenS2P to the systems you already run.

  • SAP connectors
  • Coupa connectors
  • REST connectors
  • Sync engine
  • Field mappings
  • Integration monitoring

Security & Administration

Enterprise-grade security is built in from the start: JWT authentication, role-based access control, tenant isolation, audit logging, security middleware, and rate limiting.

Roles supported out of the box

  • System Admin
  • Procurement Admin
  • Buyer
  • Requester
  • Approver
  • Supplier User
  • Finance User
