What's Next

v0.8 — AI Intelligence

With the Enterprise MVP baseline shipped in v0.7.1, the next release layers AI on top of every core workflow — turning data that's already in the platform into decisions and recommendations.

v0.7.1 Enterprise MVP Roadmap Previous Releases

Supplier Intelligence AI

Turn supplier data already in the platform into an early-warning system and a scorecard, automatically.

  • Supplier risk scoring
  • Performance insights
  • Risk recommendations

Contract AI

Read contracts at scale — summarized, flagged, and searchable.

  • Contract summarization
  • Clause analysis
  • Risk detection

Spend Advisor

Surface savings hiding in spend data that would otherwise take an analyst days to find.

  • Savings opportunities
  • Duplicate detection
  • Category insights

Procurement Copilot

A natural-language assistant for procurement teams — ask a question, get an answer, without learning a query language or hunting through dashboards.

  • "Show high-risk suppliers"
  • "Find savings opportunities"
  • "Explain pending approvals"

Looking further ahead

v0.8 builds directly on the Source-to-Pay foundation shipped in v0.7.1. For the longer-term vision — from initial platform setup through general availability — see the full platform roadmap on the homepage, or the detailed technical roadmap on GitHub.
